GDPR Compliance

Compliance concerning accordance with Regulation EU 2016⁄679

General Data Protection Regulation enters into force at May 25, 2018. This GDPR compliance shall understood as unilaterally binding upon us and become operational as an exhibit to the Terms and Conditions. LiveChat.   1. Purpose
    1. This policy codifies our commitment to the protection of data information and security across the enterprise. Data protection commitment is necessary for LiveChat, Inc. to maintain business operations and customer trust and shall be binding upon LiveChat, Inc. Services including those available through www.livechatinc.com, www.botengine.ai, www.chat.io, www.knowledgebase.ai.

    3. As this Compliance form is unilaterally binding upon LiveChat, Inc. it may be used by any party using LiveChat, Inc. Services as a proof for our declaration concerning GDPR compliance.
  2. Scope of application
    1. This policy statement applies to all LiveChat, Inc. operations all over the system internally (including all in-house software solutions) and externally to all third-parties who may access to LiveChat, Inc. systems and data.
  3. Compliance
    1. LiveChat. Inc, employees and Sub-processors who may have access to LiveChat, Inc. IT systems and data, regardless of role or responsibilities, shares in the responsibility for assuring that LiveChat, Inc. Services are conducted in a manner that satisfies requirements of European data protection laws.
