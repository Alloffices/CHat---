Frequently asked questions

What is BotEngine?

BotEngine is a natural language understanding framework that allows you to create intelligent chat bot that will communicate with your customers. You can use it with platforms like Slack, Facebook, LiveChat and Kik. In the future, we’ll support more 3rd party services and tools.

Where can I find documentation on your API?

Here you can find BotEngine documentation: https://botengine.ai/docs.

Do I have to implement my bot to test it?

Nope. You can test it live inside the application.

What is a chat bot?

Chat bot is an AI-based service which can communicate with your customers independently and without the involvement of an ‘alive’ representative. You can use chat bot to handle all kinds of simple situations like scheduling a meeting or processing an order.
