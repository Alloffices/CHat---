Frequently Asked Questions

What is a paid conversation?

Successful interaction between the bot and the client is considered as a paid conversation, regardless the length of the chat.

You won't be charged for ineffective chats with default interactions only or tests made in the built-in BotEngine console chat.

What payment forms do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, such as: Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover.

Can I use my chat bot on several websites, fan pages or messaging services?

Sure! Feel free to use your chat bots wherever you need.

Is there any contract required?

No. There are no contracts or hidden fees. You are being charged only for the amount of conversations you start each month. You can cancel anytime.

How many chat bots can I create?

You can create as many chat bots as you want at no additional fees.

How can I get an invoice/a receipt?

We will email you an invoice or a receipt at the provided email address after each successful payment.

Do I need to provide my credit card details to register?

To start using BotEngine you need to only give us your email address. Payment details are necessary to continue using BotEngine after the 14-days trial expires.

What happens after the trial ends?

At the end of a trial, your data and setup remains intact. You can login in and add your payment details to continue.
