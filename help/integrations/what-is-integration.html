What are integrations

luiza jurczyk

Last update: 31st of Jul 2018

BotEngine is a universal chatbot framework that easily integrates with popular messaging apps.

You can create the story you want and use it with your favorite apps or add to your website with just a few straightforward steps. Moreover, you can use the same story with many platforms and places.

BotEngine integrates with the following apps:

  • Facebook Messenger
  • Slack
  • LiveChat
  • chat.io

Additionally, we’ve prepared for you also a widget that you can very quickly implement on your website. Select Chat Widget from the integrations panel. Choose from many various features to personalize the widget and adjust its look to your business.

Upcoming integrations

We’re constantly working to bring more integrations to BotEngine based on your feedback. Please go to the Awaiting Integrations section to vote for the integration you. We depend our development process on your votes so it may speed up the process.
