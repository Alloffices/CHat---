How to clone your Story

luiza jurczyk

Last update: 10th of Sep 2018

Clone your stories and build new comprehensive chatbots on the top of them. You can also use the clone as a safe copy of your work.

The duplicating process copies all the necessary elements of your story including all interactions, entities, and webhooks.

Remember that the copied story won't be integrated with any application even if the primary story is.

How to clone a story

  1. Go to your Dashboard.
  2. Click on the settings button of the story you want to clone. We’ll be cloning the Small Talk story.
  3. Select Duplicate Story.

  4. Now you can find your duplicated story on your Dashboard. The name of our clone is Small Talk 2.
  5. The clone is ready. You can start working on it or leave it for later.
