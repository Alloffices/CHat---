What are References

luiza jurczyk

Last update: 14th of Sep 2018

With References, you can use an existing interaction within another point of your story. The interaction will keep all of its setup and heritage as well.

Keep in mind that by editing the referred interaction, you are also changing the initial one. References works more like a magic shortcuts between parts of your story rather than a cloning tool.
